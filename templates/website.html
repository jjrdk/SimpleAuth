Why choose SimpleIdServer ?

SimpleIdServer is an end to end security solution used to manage the software permissions.

  • Compliant with new security standards : OPENID, OAUTH2.0, SCIM2.0, UMA2.0
  • Open source
  • Advanced tools : ui administration, identities provisioning etc ...

For who ?

You're a software architect and you whish to :

  • Manage the permissions of your existing or new applications
  • Expose the applications to the public and protect them
  • Give the control to your end users on the permissions management of their resources

What's SimpleIdServer ?

Authenticate your end-users
  • Authenticate the end users in a website / api or mobile application
  • Use Two factor authentication
  • Use passwordless authentication
Manage the authorization
  • Protect your APIs from unauthorized applications
  • Protect the resources owned by your end-users
  • Share protected resources to other end-users
Manage the end-users identities
  • Manage the identities of your end-users
  • Provision the end-users identities from externals sources such as Active directory

Use cases

  • Add user provisioning with an Active Directory
  • Protect and share Office documents without dependencies on online storage solution such as : DropBox or OneDrive
  • As a patient I want to grant access to my person data to a doctor or public organisation
